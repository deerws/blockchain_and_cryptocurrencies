\begin{primarybox}{The Opportunity}
\textbf{USD 50B+} locked in DeFi lending, with credit assessment that has not evolved since 2018.

\textbf{40--60 fintechs globally} are actively trying to launch undercollateralized lending products and need credit scoring infrastructure they cannot build alone.

\textbf{No incumbent owns this market.} Existing on-chain scoring projects offer one-shot scores; none provide continuous monitoring or verifiable on-chain anchoring.
\end{primarybox}

\columnbreak

\begin{tealbox}{The Solution}
\textbf{ChainScore} reads any Ethereum wallet's full transaction history, applies a multi-agent ML pipeline, and returns a credit score (0--1000) in seconds.

\textbf{Continuous monitoring:} agents watch wallets and emit alerts when risk profiles change.

\textbf{Verifiable anchoring:} every score is hashed to Ethereum, allowing any party to audit the underlying data.

\textbf{B2B REST API} delivered as SaaS to fintechs and digital banks.
\end{tealbox}

\end{multicols}

% ─── How it works ──────────────────────────────────────────
\begin{tealbox}{How It Works --- in 4 Steps}
\small
\begin{tabularx}{\linewidth}{@{}p{0.4cm}X@{}}
\textbf{1.} & \textbf{Send a wallet address.} Your backend calls our REST API with a single Ethereum address. \\
\textbf{2.} & \textbf{We analyze the full history.} Our agents examine every transaction, protocol interaction, collateral position and liquidation event for that wallet. \\
\textbf{3.} & \textbf{Receive a score and risk breakdown.} A number from 0 to 1000 plus the top three factors driving it, in under 800 ms. \\
\textbf{4.} & \textbf{Subscribe to alerts.} Optional webhooks fire when a borrower's score drops, before they default. \\
\end{tabularx}
\end{tealbox}
